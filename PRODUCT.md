# Product

## Register

product

## Users

- **Buyers**: consumers discovering and purchasing artisanal mezcal; may be new to mezcal or experienced connoisseurs
- **Sellers**: mezcal producers selling direct; need to manage products, inventory, and orders
- **Admins**: marketplace operators monitoring catalog, disputes, compliance, and platform health
- **Context**: Marketplace for artisanal mezcal from Oaxaca; users expect heritage, authenticity, and quality provenance

## Product Purpose

A marketplace platform that connects mezcal producers directly to consumers, enabling authentic discovery and commerce while preserving the artisanal heritage and producer identity that defines the spirit.

## Brand Personality

**Artisanal with a premium touch.** Trustworthy, rooted in craft and tradition, yet refined and accessible. The marketplace celebrates producer stories and product provenance without gatekeeping expertise.

## Anti-references

- Overly modern/minimalist minimalism that erases cultural heritage (should feel grounded in Oaxaca/mezcal tradition)
- Generic e-commerce templates (should feel unique and intentional)
- Cheap/discount aesthetics (this is premium, not budget)
- Corporate or sterile interfaces (should feel human and authentic)

## Design Principles

1. **Craft and provenance first** — celebrate producer stories, batch numbers, region, and heritage; never bury sourcing info
2. **Premium but approachable** — accessible to newcomers; doesn't gatekeep or lecture; respects expertise
3. **Heritage visible** — Oaxacan and mezcal culture should feel present in tone, imagery, and rhythm; not just in colors
4. **Trust through transparency** — show producer profiles, sourcing, batch details, reviews; users know who made what they're buying
5. **Mobile-first, no compromise** — mobile buyers get the full experience: rich imagery, clear navigation, full product context

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG AA minimum** across all surfaces
- **Bilingual** (Spanish + English); no assumptions about locale
- **Color-blind safe palettes** — do not rely on color alone to signal status (badges, active states, errors)
- **Reduced motion** — animations respect `prefers-reduced-motion`; no auto-playing content
- **Keyboard navigation** — all interactive elements reachable without a mouse
